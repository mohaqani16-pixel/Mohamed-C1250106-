How to Use AirWave

🧭 1. How to Navigate the Site

The AirWave website features a consistent navigation bar at the top of every page. Here's how to use it:

  • Home: Returns you to the main page with the featured episode player and latest episodes
  • Episodes: Takes you to the gallery page where you can browse all episodes and view the episode table
  • Book a Guest: Opens the guest booking form if you want to appear on the podcast
  • Help: Brings you to this help page with instructions and FAQs
  • About: Shows information about the podcast, host, and creator

Tip: The navigation bar is sticky and stays at the top as you scroll, so you can always access it.

🎵 2. How to Use the Audio Player

The audio player on the Home page lets you listen to featured episodes. Here's how each control works:

  • Play/Pause Button (▶/⏸): Click to start or pause the episode playback
  • Progress Bar: Shows how much of the episode has played. You can click or drag the slider to jump to any part of the episode
  • Current Time: Displays how many minutes and seconds have elapsed (e.g., 2:30 means 2 minutes 30 seconds)
  • Duration: Shows the total length of the episode (e.g., 45:30 means 45 minutes 30 seconds)
  • Volume Control (🔊): Drag the volume slider left or right to decrease or increase the volume

Tip: The player remembers your volume setting, so you don't need to adjust it every time.

📀 3. How to Read the Episode Table and Gallery

The Gallery page (Episodes) has two main sections:

Episode Gallery Grid:

  • Each episode is displayed as a card with cover art, title, and description
  • Hover over any card to see a subtle highlight effect
  • The grid is responsive and adjusts to your screen size

Episode Data Table:

  • Episode #: The unique episode number (e.g., 001, 002)
  • Title: The name of the episode topic
  • Guest: The name of the featured guest speaker
  • Duration: How long the episode is (in minutes:seconds)
  • Release Date: When the episode was published

Tip: Alternate rows in the table have a different background color to make reading easier. Hover over any row to highlight it.

📝 4. How to Fill Out the Guest Booking Form

To apply as a guest on AirWave, follow these steps:

  1. Personal Information:
    • Enter your full name (required)
    • Provide a valid email address (required)
    • Add your phone number (optional)
  2. Expertise & Topic:
    • Select your area of expertise from the dropdown menu
    • Specify the exact topic you want to discuss
  3. Appearance Preferences:
    • Select your gender (required)
    • Check all areas of interest that apply (Live Interview, Pre-recorded, Remote Call, or Studio Visit)
  4. Additional Information:
    • Write a pitch explaining why you'd be a great guest
    • Optionally add your website or social media links
    • Agree to the terms and conditions
  5. Submit: Click "Submit Application" to send your booking request

Tip: Fields marked with * are required. You cannot submit the form without completing them.

📧 5. How to Use the Subscribe Form

The subscribe form appears in the footer of every page. Here's how to use it:

  1. Scroll down to the bottom of any page
  2. Find the "Subscribe to AirWave" section
  3. Enter your email address in the input field
  4. Click the "Subscribe" button
  5. You'll receive a confirmation message

After subscribing, you'll receive email notifications whenever we publish new episodes. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email.

Tip: Use a valid email address. We'll send a confirmation email to verify your subscription.
