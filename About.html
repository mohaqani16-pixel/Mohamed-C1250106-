Meet the Host

Mohamed osman

Host & Creator, AirWave Podcast

As a Software Engineering professional based in Mogadishu, Somalia, I created AirWave to bridge the gap between quality journalism and accessible media. With a passion for storytelling and technology, I bring you weekly episodes covering news, culture, business, and innovation from both local and global perspectives.

My mission is to create a platform where diverse voices can be heard, where complex topics are explained simply, and where listeners can stay informed while being entertained. Whether you're interested in technology trends, political analysis, or cultural stories, AirWave has something for everyone.

Technical Credits

This website was built entirely with vanilla web technologies as a university final project:

  • HTML5: Semantic markup with proper accessibility features (ARIA roles, labels, alt text)
  • CSS3: Flexbox layouts, responsive design, visual effects (gradients, shadows, transitions)
  • JavaScript: Vanilla JS for audio player controls, form validation, and interactivity
  • Deployment: Hosted on GitHub Pages for public access
